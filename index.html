Screen‑free • Movement‑first • Ages 5+

Math that kids can grab, twist, and pat

Fermaths is a hands‑on toy that turns arithmetic into quick, playful actions. Think of it like a classic call‑and‑response game—but focused on numbers, quick mental math, and problem solving. No screens. Just fun.

Get in touch How it works
1 • Listen

Hear a math prompt

"Add 3!" "10+5?" "Find the even number!" Fermaths calls out playful challenges appropriate to the level you set and language.

2 • Move

React with actions

Twist in order to get the answer and pat! Physical actions map to answers, keeping little hands busy and brains engaged.

3 • Learn

Build number sense

Short rounds, instant feedback, and joyful sounds create sticky learning moments—without screens.
